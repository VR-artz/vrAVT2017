Spotlight Artists

June 1-August 31

This is a biannual show where we show off some of the incredible works of the artist within the community.

Laurent Durieux

June 1-July 31

Laurent Durieux beautiful work takes poster art to a high level. The images, which are stunningly executed, express ideas and themes of the movies he has chosen in new terms. They communicate much without words, and stand alongside the wonderful tradition of illustrative art.

Olly Moss

June 1-July 31

Oliver Jonathan "Olly" Moss is an English graphic artist, best known for his reimagining of movie posters. His work has been released by Mondo and is regularly featured in Empire magazine.

Jamie Hewlett

October 1-November 30

Jamie Christopher Hewlett is an English comic creator, animator, cover designer, illustrator, music video director, and songwriter, best known as the co-creator of the comic book Tank Girl and the virtual band Gorillaz, alongside Blur frontman Damon Albarn.
